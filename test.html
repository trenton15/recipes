Breakfast Recipes

  • Blueberry Pancakes

    Ingredients: flour, sugar, blueberries, eggs, milk, baking powder, salt

    Instructions: Mix ingredients together, cook on a griddle, and enjoy!

  • Scrambled Eggs

    Ingredients: eggs, milk, salt, pepper, butter

    Instructions: Whisk eggs, milk, salt, and pepper together. Heat butter in a pan and add the egg mixture. Cook until scrambled and serve.

Lunch Recipes

  • Tuna Salad Sandwich

    Ingredients: tuna, mayonnaise, celery, onion, bread, lettuce, tomato

    Instructions: Mix tuna, mayonnaise, celery, and onion together. Spread mixture on bread and top with lettuce and tomato. Enjoy!

  • Caprese Salad

    Ingredients: tomatoes, mozzarella, basil, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, pepper

    Instructions: Slice tomatoes and mozzarella. Arrange on a plate with basil leaves. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar. Season with salt and pepper. Enjoy!
