Yellow Chicken Curry

Ingredients

1 Tbsp coconut oil
4 Tbsp yellow curry paste
2 15-oz cans of coconut milk
2 cups chicken stock
4 carrots, peeled and chopped
4 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and chopped
1 yellow onion, sliced into wedges, then halved
2 chicken breasts, very thinly sliced
11‚ÅÑ2 tsp fish sauce (optional)
3/4 tsp salt
1 1/2 tsp sugar (plus more to taste)
Cilantro for garnish

Instructions

-Boil potatoes until fork-tender. Drain and set aside.
-In a large pot, heat up coconut oil. Add curry paste to oil, stir for a few minutes. Add in coconut milk and chicken stock. Stir and bring to boil.
-Add in carrots and onions. Once carrots are almost fork-tender, add in thinly sliced chicken and cook for two or three minutes.
-Remove from heat. Add in potatoes. Stir in fish sauce, salt, and sugar. Serve over rice and garnish with cilantro.
