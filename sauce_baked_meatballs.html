Sauce-Baked Meatballs

Ingredients

1 egg
1⁄2 c milk or water
1'c rolled oats
1⁄2 c chopped onion
1/4 t garlic powder
1/4 t pepper
1 t chili powder
11⁄2 lb ground beef
1 t salt

3/4 c ketchup
3/4 c brown sugar
2 t liquid smoke (opt.)
1/4 t garlic powder
1 t Worcestershire sauce

Instructions

Mix all together and form into meatballs. (I like to use a scoop - the medium scoop makes 33 meatballs) Can be cooked now or frozen (single layer) on a cookie sheet. I usually make 4x batch, freeze then store in Ziploc. If cooked from frozen, takes 1 1⁄2 hrs in oven.

Mix together and pour over meatballs in casserole dish. Bake, covered, for 1 hour at 350.
